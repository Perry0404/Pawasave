## Events of default
Non-payment after grace; insolvency; breach of covenant; material misrepresentation;
abandonment of TGE / token plan; change of control without consent.

## Remedies
On default, Lender may: suspend further drawdowns; accelerate outstanding amounts;
exercise the token warrant / claim the escrowed allocation; and/or record a write-off.
(On-chain, the credit line is suspended and, if uncollectible, written off.)
