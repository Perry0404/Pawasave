PawaSave Lending

On-chain credit for crypto-native teams · built on Base

PawaSave runs a cNGN lending protocol with two borrowing tracks: an over-collateralized money market for liquid collateral, and structured credit lines for institutions and pre-TGE projects. Loans settle in cNGN (compliant Naira stablecoin) on Base.

Status: the protocol is completing its second security audit, after which it enters beta. Initial facilities are measured and scale with track record.

Track 2 — Structured credit line (institutional & pre-TGE)

A relationship-based revolving facility. We onboard your project, agree a credit limit and a fixed APR, and you draw and repay over the term. Uncollateralized — credit is extended on the strength of the project, the agreement, and (for pre-TGE) token upside.

Pre-TGE projects

The token isn't liquid yet, so we don't price it as collateral. The token side is structured as upside, not security:

  • A token warrant or discounted TGE allocation, held in escrow
  • Drawdowns can be milestone-based
  • Repayment from revenue, treasury, or the raise

Listing-fee financing

A short bridge loan sized to your exchange listing / market-making cost, repaid at or shortly after TGE from listing/raise proceeds — secured by a contractual claim on a slice of the TGE allocation.

Why borrow from PawaSave

  • Speed — on-chain settlement on Base, no bank rails
  • Flexibility — collateralized or relationship credit; you pick the track
  • Aligned — for pre-TGE we take token upside, so we win when you do
  • Compliant rails — cNGN is a regulated Naira stablecoin
