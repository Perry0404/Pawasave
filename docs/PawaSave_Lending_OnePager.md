# PawaSave Lending — Borrower One-Pager

**On-chain credit for crypto-native teams, built on Base.**
PawaSave runs a cNGN lending protocol with two borrowing tracks: an over-collateralized
money market for liquid collateral, and structured credit lines for institutions and
pre-TGE projects. Loans settle in **cNGN** (compliant Naira stablecoin) on **Base**.

> Status: the protocol is completing its **second security audit**, after which it enters
> **beta**. Initial facilities are measured and scale with track record.

---

## Track 1 — Collateralized borrowing (live)

Borrow cNGN instantly against liquid collateral. Fully on-chain, non-custodial.

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Collateral accepted | USDC (75% LTV), USDT (75% LTV), cNGN (60% LTV) |
| Borrow asset | cNGN (Base) |
| Interest | Variable, market-driven (jump-rate model) |
| Loan term | 30 / 90 / 180 days (4-day grace) |
| Origination fee | 0.5% flat |
| Early repayment | Free, anytime |
| Default | Liquidation if collateral health breaks or loan is overdue |
| Limit | ₦50M per user (retail); institutional limits set per account |

**Best for:** teams holding stablecoins/treasury who want working capital without selling.

## Track 2 — Structured credit line (institutional & pre-TGE)

A relationship-based revolving facility. We onboard your project, agree a credit limit
and a fixed APR, and you draw and repay over the term. **Uncollateralized** — credit is
extended on the strength of the project, agreement, and (for pre-TGE) a token upside.

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Facility type | Revolving credit line, uncollateralized |
| Borrow asset | cNGN (Base); other settlement by arrangement |
| Pricing | Fixed APR, set per partner at onboarding |
| Draw / repay | Flexible within the agreed limit over the facility term |
| Min ticket | ~$10k equivalent |
| Max | Set per deal after diligence (no fixed ceiling) |
| Onboarding | KYB + facility agreement; managed-custody option (no key/gas needed) |

### Pre-TGE projects
The token isn't liquid yet, so **we don't price it as collateral**. Instead the token side
is structured as **upside, not security**:
- A **token warrant** or **discounted TGE allocation** to PawaSave, held in escrow.
- Drawdowns can be **milestone-based**.
- Repayment from revenue, treasury, or the raise.

### Listing-fee financing
A short **bridge loan** sized to your exchange listing/market-making cost, repaid at or
shortly after **TGE** from listing/raise proceeds. Secured by a contractual claim on a
slice of the TGE allocation.
